What is this?

This is a tiny starter template for when you know you should make something simple, but your brain keeps trying to architect a full SaaS platform with dark mode, user auth, and seventeen modals.

Instead: one page, a few sections, a sprinkle of JavaScript, and enough structure that you can plug in your own content without fighting the CSS.

Why you might actually use this

🧱 Stupidly simple

One HTML file, one CSS file, one JS file. No bundlers, no build step, no 900MB node_modules folder silently judging you.

🎭 Built-in personality

Fun microcopy, playful hover states, and a tiny mood system to remind you this is supposed to be enjoyable, not existential.

🛠 Easy to reskin

Swap the colors, change the font, rename everything, claim you coded it at 3AM in a single sitting. I support you.
